BUTTER SCOTCH

  • Butterscotch is a confection made from brown sugar and butter that's known for its rich, smooth texture and sweet flavor
  • It can be enjoyed in many forms, including hard candies, sauces, and flavored desserts
  • Butterscotch has a deeper flavor and darker color than caramel because it's made with brown sugar instead of white sugar
  • Butterscotch can be used as a sauce for ice cream, in baking for cookies and bars, or as individually wrapped candies.
