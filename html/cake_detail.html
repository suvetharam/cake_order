VANILLA

  • A vanilla cake is a classic and beloved dessert that is enjoyed around the world.
  • It is a simple cake that is made from basic ingredients such as flour, eggs, sugar, and butter, and flavored with vanilla extract.
  • Vanilla cake is incredibly versatile, and can be served alone, with a simple glaze or frosting, or with a variety of toppings and fillings.
  • Whether you're serving it at a party or just as a simple treat, a vanilla cake is sure to please.
