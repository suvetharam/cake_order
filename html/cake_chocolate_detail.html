CHOCOLATE

  • Chocolate cake is a classic dessert made with cocoa powder, sugar, flour, eggs, butter, and oil, and baked until soft and fluffy.
  • It's a popular choice for celebrations like birthdays, anniversaries, and festivals.
  • Chocolate cake can also include other ingredients like fudge, vanilla creme, and other sweeteners
